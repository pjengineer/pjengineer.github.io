Brief Bio

Pinalkumar Engineer is an Assistant Professor in Electronics Engineering Department at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT) Surat.

His research interests include Edge Computing, Application Specific Processor Design, Energy-efficient Computing, VLSI architecture for real-time signal/image processing, High performance embedded computing.
